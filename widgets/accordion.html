The giraffe is a large African hoofed mammal belonging to the genus Giraffa. It is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth. Traditionally, giraffes have been thought of as one species, Giraffa camelopardalis, with nine subspecies.

There are more than 40 million donkeys in the world, mostly in underdeveloped countries, where they are used principally as draught or pack animals. While working donkeys are often associated with those living at or below subsistence, small numbers of donkeys or asses are kept for breeding or as pets in developed countries.

Kangaroos are marsupials from the family Macropodidae (macropods, meaning "large foot"). In common use the term is used to describe the largest species from this family, the red kangaroo, as well as the antilopine kangaroo, eastern grey kangaroo, and western grey kangaroo.

Guidelines


  • Keyboard
    • Trigger responds to Enter or Space keys
    • After being activated, the trigger does not lose focus
  • Name, role, value, other
    • The trigger has a unique, descriptive and succinct name
    • The trigger that opens it is a button. It could also have the button role (role=”button”)
    • Button has aria-expanded=”true” when panel is visible, aria-expanded=”false” when not
    • The button announces what panel it controls via aria-controls=”id of the panel” (for bonus points)
    • Disclosed panels do not display with excessive animation
