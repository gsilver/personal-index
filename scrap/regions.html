Giving complex views semantics.

The use case was Canvas discussions. The goal is to provide a lightweight structure that will give screen reader users a good context for all the components of a post:

  • How is this post related to others?
  • What does this "Reply" button do? What am I going to reply to?

There are various possible mechanisms. Let's experiment!
