Bookmarklets for vendor demos

Installing bookmarklets: drag the link to the bookmarks bar of your browser. For best results use Chrome. You can create a folder on your bookmark bar and drag the links to that to keep things compact.

Using bookmarklets: click on the bookmarklet in your bookmark bar on in the folder.
